# Welcome

This online book compiles a collection of **R-based tutorials** across several topic areas:

- Foundations (R, RStudio, Quarto, Git/GitHub)
- Data science workflows
- Machine learning
- Causal inference
- Economic evaluation and decision sciences
- Simulation models
- Health policy applications
- Statistics and uncertainty

Each topic area is a **part** of the book, and each tutorial is a **separate Quarto file**.
This makes it easy to add, modify, or reuse individual tutorials.

Use the sidebar to navigate by part and select a specific tutorial.
